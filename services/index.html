Services

We offer our experience in more than 12 years of co-development, porting, and optimization for games.

We offer our in-depth know-how and years of experience in game development to international clients. We take on game development projects as a whole – from concept to deployment – as well as parts of larger projects in a manner of co-development or classical outsourcing. While providing the full range of game development, our team mainly focuses on technical engineering support, from porting and optimization to gameplay systems and backend solutions.

Let us know about your needs at . We’re looking forward to working with you!

Small, flexible, and international

We managed to distill the professional expertise of game development into a small and highly skilled team that can flexibly adapt to the required workload. The company is located in Austria and is experienced in distributed collaborations with international clients, partners, and team members. Our team has 10+ years of experience with a vast range of engines and platforms.

Game development

We offer the management and execution of the complete game creation process. We design and develop games in an agile and iterative manner, which enables us to tie in the clients at regular intervals to guarantee satisfaction. Starting with the concept phase, where a given idea is reviewed, or drawn from scratch, the development covers all necessary aspects, including iterations in close collaboration with our clients, and thorough testing right from the start. We have established build and deployment pipelines to all major platforms and conveniently provide builds to our clients’ doorstep with our in-house distribution service called “Airship”.

Examples: Schein , Rebuilders , Grattle

Engineering

Be it the first prototype, or a port of an existing game to a new platform – our development team is happy to help out. We have experience in console and PC development as well as mobile and XR. Our portfolio includes titles featuring local singleplayer and network multiplayer games, projects optimized for a single platform as well as multi-platform sim-ship releases. We don’t shy away from technical challenges and welcome your custom-made or highly bizarre game engine.

Examples: Invincible Presents: Atom Eve , Luther - the Journey
Web and backend: Phaser, Babylon.js, three.js, Angular, PHP, MySQL, PostgreSQL

Porting

We have ported games based on various engines to consoles, mobile devices and web browsers. We provide a comprehensive porting process starting with a preliminary evaluation up to the final certification and submission process.

Examples: SOUTH PARK: SNOW DAY! , The Settlers Online

Optimization

We love to take a look under the hood of your game to find out what is troubling the CPU, GPU or memory. Our past projects targeting mobile devices or last-gen consoles taught us valuable skills in this regard. Our suite of profiling tools for various engines helped us improve the performance of every project so far.

Examples: SOUTH PARK: SNOW DAY! , On Your Tail

Superluminal Nsight Pix Live++ RenderDoc

Remasters

We love classic games and are happy to help you in your endeavour to revive them; so far no code base was too old or too obscure for us to bring it back to life. You might also want to know that one of our traits is to port old graphic APIs to consoles, so your game may shine in a new light on more platforms.

Art & Animation

Is your game project in need of graphical assets? We are happy to supply concept art, 3D models, animations and textures.
