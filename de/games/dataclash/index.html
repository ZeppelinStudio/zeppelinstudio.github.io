Puzzled by all the talk about the GDPR? Make use of your rights to protect your privacy in the fast paced tower defence browser game Data Clash. Defend your data packages from tech giants and data kraken and fight for the top spot in the global leader board.

The so-called General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) became effective in Europe on 25 May 2018. As the name suggests, it is supposed to ensure the rights to protect one’s data and privacy. However, the sheer volume of 11 chapters and 99 articles naturally leads to a lot of confusion for citizens and companies alike. The browser game Data Clash gives you a compact, simple, and foremost fun overview of all the new consumer rights and the competent management of their data. Data Clash is part of the nationwide information campaign “Your Data – Your Rights” by Digitale Gesellschaft e.V. in Germany.

Key Features

  • Fun, straight forward and fast paced tower defense action
  • Playful education of a complex but highly relevant topic
  • Global ranking and highscores

Production

“Data Clash” was designed by the specialists for learning games and gamification Ijsfontein Interactive Media in collaboration with Target Games GmbH and Zeppelin Studio. Zeppelin Studio managed and executed the technical development of the game. As an HTML5 application using the Phaser 2 framework it can be played on all common desktop and mobile browsers.
