Wildshade

Saddle your horse, ready your magic potions, and head to the starting line – will you become the next champion of Wildshade?

Google Play
App Store

Wildshade is an online multiplayer horse racing game for mobile devices. Dive into this fantastic world full of magic. Bond with your favorite horse, and compete with your friends in wild races through forests, castles, or snowy hills.

  • Manage your horse stables and breed your perfect racehorse.
  • Compete for the podium on a multitude of gorgeous racetracks.
  • Hone your magic skills, time them right, and leave your opponents far behind.
  • Race through the campaign mode and unlock all kinds of gear for your horse.
  • Climb the ranks in the global ladder. Can you make it to the prestigious Diamond League?

Production

The international mobile game publisher and developer Tivola has a long history of releasing successful animal-focused games. As Wildshade was aiming to become their 2021 flagship title, Zeppelin Studio joined the project in collaboration with our partners at anti entropy to tackle the game’s backend development. Together we connected the Unity-based mobile client with Microsoft’s backend environments PlayFab and Azure. By integrating these services, we made the seamless live management of users, features, store offers, and analytics possible. After the initial release we continued our cooperation and released several competitive features and content updates to a growing and passionate community.
