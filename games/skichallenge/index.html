Alpine skiing holds a significant place in Austrian culture, which is why ski racing games tend to enjoy great popularity. “Ski Challenge” has been a prominent IP since its first release in 2004, captivating players worldwide in digital competitions to achieve the fastest times down the slope. Following the discontinuation of development in 2016, players endured years without official Ski Challenge competitions. However, this has changed now: In collaboration with Greentube and Mi'pu'mi Games , we’ve revived this classic game with a complete reboot for mobile devices. Embark on a quest for speed on renowned tracks such as Zermatt, Kitzbühel, Garmisch, and many more. Compete against friends and foes in a multi-tier leaderboard and watch your competition fall behind.

Key Features

  • Visit world-famous ski areas and race on official tracks.
  • Unlock stylish and silly gear to customize your avatar.
  • Battle for the high score with 6 leaderboard tiers.
  • Represent your country in the global Nations Cup.
  • Join the active community and events on Discord and in real life.
  • Participate in regular season updates with custom challenges and new content.

Production

Our team partnered with Greentube and Mi'pu'mi Games to bring the classic IP to modern mobile devices. The development was based on the original resource material while undergoing a massive overhaul to remaster the game in the newest Unity engine version, with a backend setup powered by PlayFab and Azure. We contributed to the project with senior generalist engineers, handling the following tasks:

  • Setup and maintenance of backend infrastructure, including live support
  • Constant and close collaboration with DevOps teams to ensure high availability
  • Content delivery pipeline to update game assets independent of full releases
  • Implementation of CI/CD pipeline and automated E2E testing
  • Integration of Apple and Google platform authentication and in-app purchasing
  • Performance tweaking and memory optimization
  • Code architecture
  • UI implementation
  • Gameplay programming
