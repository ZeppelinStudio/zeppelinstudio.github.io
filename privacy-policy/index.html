It does not govern player data, which is handled under separate game-specific privacy policies.

4. Recipients of Personal Data

We may share your data with:

  • Authorized staff involved in handling your query or application

All third-party processors are bound by GDPR-compliant agreements to protect your data.

5. Data Transfers Outside the EEA

If we transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions are in place.

We use services provided by Google LLC (including Google Workspace, Gmail, and Google Drive) to support our internal communication and document management. In doing so, Google LLC may process personal data on our behalf as a data processor. These services are governed by data processing agreements that incorporate the Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other safeguards in accordance with the GDPR. For more information on Google’s data protection practices, please refer to Google Cloud’s GDPR resource center .

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data:

  • For up to 10 years after the last interaction or application, unless legal requirements necessitate a longer period
  • Immediately upon request for deletion, where permitted under GDPR
