Zeppelin Studio is hiring!

Join us aboard the Zeppelin, become part of our small game-dev team, and help us grow!

Web Developer – Intermediate to Senior Level (div/f/m)

This position has been filled.

Do you enjoy developing front and back end components that seamlessly intertwine to run in harmony ever after? Then it’s YOU we’re looking for! Under the hood, most of our games are powered by a content management system and back end. These systems handle our players’ rankings and tournaments, manage and provide sessions for multiplayer games, or allow editors to create live game content in custom-built web interfaces.

The position is a full-time employment with 38.5-hour work weeks, a minimum gross salary of 44,660 EUR per year, and yearly 20 days of paid vacation according to the Austrian IT collective agreement. While remote collaborations are a daily part of our work, we value regular personal contact and are looking for a new team member who lives in, or is willing to move to, the metropolitan area of Vienna. Even though we prefer a full-time employee, we are open to discussions about reduced working hours.

WHAT WE OFFER

We offer a friendly and well-equipped work environment and ensure that all team members have access to the tech they need – be it in the office or at home. We are a young, lively and fun-loving team; we enjoy working hard and yet we highly value a good work-life balance, so we do what we can to avoid crunch times (so far quite successfully!). We also believe in the importance of personal growth and regularly offer opportunities for individual development, further education, and game jams.

Most of our projects are co-developments for our long-term partners, where we either fully or partially take over the programming aspects of their game projects. Nevertheless, we also strive to develop our own games and IPs, to which all interested team members can contribute.

Join us aboard the Zeppelin, become part of our small game-dev team, and help us grow!

If you would like to know more about us, please check out our home page at zeppelinstudio.net

WHAT WE WOULD LIKE YOU TO DO

Programming web interfaces in Angular Writing APIs in PHP Setting up databases and migrations Maintaining our Docker setup and improving it further WHAT WE EXPECT YOU TO HAVE Experience in Java/Typescript Experience with PHP Experience with SQL Fluency in English and/or German (speech & writing) Working knowledge of GIT Bonus: Experience with Docker Experience with Phaser, Babylon or Godot Experience with PlayFab and Azure Functions Experience with game engines

HOW TO APPLY If you are still reading, we hope that means you are interested in this position! Please send your application letter in English or German to jobs@zeppelinstudio.net. We encourage you to attach a portfolio of your work, since we believe a track record is more important than certificates or grades on paper.
